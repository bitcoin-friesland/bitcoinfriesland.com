About the BTC Map

The Bitcoin Map shows all businesses and locations in Friesland that accept Bitcoin payments. This interactive map helps you easily find places where you can pay with Bitcoin.

The map is regularly updated with new businesses that accept Bitcoin. Help us make the map complete by reporting new Bitcoin-friendly businesses.

How to use the map?

  • Click on the map to zoom in on a specific area
  • Use the search function to find a specific city or business
  • Click on the markers to see more information about a business
  • Use the filters to search by payment type (Lightning, On-chain)
